Immersive Worlds Through Trade

Breath life into your worlds through rich economies driven by every NPC and player. Make societies that rise, fall, and migrate based on market forces. Is a lich ravaging the fields of the North? Get ready for mass emigration to the South.

No More Dumb Shopkeepers

Tired of your shopkeepers buying their 100th dragon bone for the same price as the first?
Did they even need those dragon bones in the first place? Ekon's advanced economics AI turns every NPC in your game into rational (or irrational) economic actors that buy, sell, and produce goods within your game's markets.
